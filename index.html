What it does

Everything a close needs

A single platform that replaces spreadsheet checklists, status email chains, and manual CFO updates — all driven by live task data.

AI status generation
GPT-4o reads the live task board and writes a formal CFO update in seconds — naming blockers, owners, and recommended escalation actions.
Kanban task board
28 close tasks tracked across Not Started, In Progress, Blocked, and Complete. Blocker notes show root cause. One-click task updates.
Close checklist
28 tasks across 5 categories with owner, due date, and blocker flag. Reusable across periods — click "New period" to reset for next month.
Analytics dashboard
Six-period trend data showing close duration, blocker root causes, completion by owner, and on-time rate by category. Identifies systemic issues.
Four views

One tool, every perspective

Different stakeholders need different views of the same close data. The tool surfaces all of them from a single data model.

01 / Task board
Kanban board
Four-column layout. Real-time task status. Blocker notes with root cause. AI status generation button. Designed for daily stand-ups.
02 / Checklist
Close checklist
Linear task list with checkboxes. Organised by close day. Owner and blocker flags. Click to mark complete. Resets for each period.
03 / Timeline
Gantt timeline
D+1 to D+7 horizontal bar chart. Tasks coloured by status. Today marker. Shows scheduling conflicts and critical path at a glance.
04 / Analytics
Trend analytics
Six-month KPI trends. Close duration vs target. Blocker root cause donut. Completion by owner bar chart. On-time rate by category.

CFO status update — Day 4 of 5

The June 2024 month-end close is progressing with 16 of 28 tasks complete (57%) as of Day 4 of the 5-day close window. The overall position is at moderate risk — three tasks are currently blocked and require immediate management intervention to meet the D+5 board pack deadline.


The most critical blocker is the balance sheet reconciliation suite, overdue since D+3, awaiting a GL extract from IT. The revenue recognition journals are also blocked pending Legal sign-off on contract amendments — this item should be escalated to the General Counsel today if approval is not received by midday. The payroll accrual confirmation remains outstanding from HR.


Seven tasks are actively in progress and on track for D+4 completion. If all three blockers are resolved today, the close remains achievable by D+5. The CFO is recommended to prioritise review of the IT escalation and Legal approval request before the afternoon stand-up.
